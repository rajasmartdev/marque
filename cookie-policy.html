Cookie Policy

Effective Date: 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how The Marque Club ("we," "us," "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described below.

1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website function properly and provide insights that help us improve the experience. Cookies can be temporary (session cookies) or remain on your device for a set period (persistent cookies).

2 How We Use Cookies

2.1 Essential Cookies

Required for Website Operation

These cookies are necessary for the website to operate correctly. They enable secure browsing, website performance, form submissions, and session management. Without these cookies, certain features of our website may not function.

2.2 Performance & Analytics Cookies

Website Usage Insights

Used to understand how visitors use the website, which pages are most popular, and how users navigate through the site. Data collected is anonymized and not personally identifiable. This helps us improve the overall user experience.

2.3 Functionality Cookies

Preference Management

Help remember your preferences, such as selected city or form choices. These cookies improve convenience and personalization but are not mandatory for the website to function.

2.4 Third-Party Cookies

External Platform Integration

Used by platforms like Instagram or LinkedIn when you interact with embedded features such as social media buttons or embedded content. We do not control these cookies, and they are subject to the respective third party's privacy policies.

3 What We Do Not Use Cookies For

We value your privacy and transparency

We do not use cookies for:

  • Advertising or marketing campaigns
  • Behavioral profiling or tracking across websites
  • Retargeting or remarketing purposes
  • Selling data to third parties
  • Automated marketing or spam

4 Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Please note: Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality and your overall user experience. Essential cookies are necessary for the website to work properly.

5 Data Retention Through Cookies

Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. They enable the website to remember you as you navigate between pages during a single session.

Persistent Cookies: These remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted. They remember your preferences and settings for future visits, improving convenience and user experience.

6 Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data practices. The updated version will show a revised "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we use cookies.
